Port Truck Network Simulation Prototype

This is a simple browser-based example of a freight traffic simulation for teaching. Trucks depart from a schematic Port of Melbourne node, choose destinations across a small network, and follow the shortest available path. You can close or reopen links, change demand and departure patterns, and see how routes, travel times, and network use change.
